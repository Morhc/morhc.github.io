SCRIPTURE

Unfortunately for myself, I find myself thinking about Christian scripture constantly, ever skeptical of what people tell me is true until I determine it for myself. I have my blog space on this site that I've been using to dump my ideas related to Scripture on it, but I really don't think that it is enough anymore for adequately documenting things. So I have decided to take on an extremely brash project that is some parts collecting what I've read and watched on Scripture and some parts a theological commentary on what to do with these messy texts. For those unaquainted with my thoughts on things, I hold a generally low view of Scripture and don't really think it has much to say to human beings living in the 21st century directly. However, I can't disentangle myself from it either. So I thought that I would do my best to provide something that attempts to think about how texts that have been considered Scripture in the Christian tradition. In the ethos of Bp. John Shelby Spong and philosopher Mark C. Taylor: this is an wanderer in the desert's attempt to err, always erring hoping to find some semblemance of truth.

Search around for my thoughts.
