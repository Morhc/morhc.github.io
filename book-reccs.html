BOOK RECOMMENDATIONS

I really enjoy reading. Probably my only hobby. My reading habits are mostly informed by my desire to live in a more equitable world and learning what I can do to contribute to that. My understanding of what that means is filtered through a Christian lens that is Presbyterian-leaning and has an eye towards liberation theology. God is the God of the oppressed, and as a Christian I feel a duty to care for the vulnerable in my local context as well as I can.

If you want to stay up to date with what I'm reading check out my Goodreads.
